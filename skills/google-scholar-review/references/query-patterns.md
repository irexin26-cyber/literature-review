# Query Patterns for Management/Economics Reviews

Use these templates to seed Scholar search:

1. **Theory + context**
   - `"dynamic capabilities" AND "digital transformation" AND SME`
2. **Mechanism search**
   - `platform governance AND (trust OR legitimacy) AND performance`
3. **Method-focused scan**
   - `(difference-in-differences OR panel data) AND ESG AND firm value`
4. **Boundary-condition scan**
   - `entrepreneurial orientation AND innovation AND (moderator OR contingency)`

Refinement loop:

1. Start broad (20-40 results).
2. Collect recurring constructs from top-cited papers.
3. Add constructs as required terms.
4. Re-run with tighter window and compare deltas.
